About me

A Legal Practioner with Eight years(8) experience. I worked with LawPavilion Business Solution Limited where I served as a Legal Officer where I was made the Team Lead in Quality Control. I also worked as a Counsel with Fola Awobusi & Co. a company baased in Ibadan where I had a knowledge in Law practice. My legal experience came into limelight when I served the Country at the Office of the Administrator General and Public Trustees in the Ministry of Justice, Ibadan.
